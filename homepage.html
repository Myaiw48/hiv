Hiv/Aids prevention in Philadelphia

Hello our names are Kareema, Aaliyah, Mya, and Jabari. We are apart of the science and math honors program at Smash Wharton. As part of this program, we designed a website focusing on an essential question; how to stop the spread of a disease in our community? This website targets young adults and teens in Philadelphia who are either experencenig HIV/AIDS or want to learn more about it. We included a hotline to provide people with an outlet to discuss their questions or concerns and seek advice. In addition, there is a resource page where you can test your knowledge by taking a quiz and be provided with more information such as clinics in Philadelphia.

Safe sex saves lifes! Don't be another statistic #stopthespread!
