HIV/AIDS prevention in Philadelphia

Hello! We are Kareema, Aaliyah, Mya, and Jabari. Our essential question is “How to stop the spread of HIV/AIDS in Philadelphia, PA?” We designed a website for young adults and teens who want to know more about HIV/AIDS We included a talk or chat hotline to ask questions or share concerns and seek advice. We also developed a resource page where you can test your knowledge by taking a quiz and find information about local clinics right here in Philadelphia.
